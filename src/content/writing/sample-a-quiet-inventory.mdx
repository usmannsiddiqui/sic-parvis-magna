Once a year I take stock of the things that do not appear on any ledger.

![A dim desk lamp over an open, half-filled notebook.](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1517842645767-c639042777db?w=1200)

Not what I produced — what I paid attention to. Who did I become while I was
busy measuring everything but myself? Draft status keeps this one out of the
build until it is ready — the honest state of most reflection.
